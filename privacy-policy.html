Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 18th March 2026

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data.

Data Handling

  • All features (highlighting, notes, tags, and summaries) operate entirely on-device.
  • No data is sent to any servers or external services.
  • All data remains stored locally in your browser.

Data Collection & Sharing

  • No personal information is collected.
  • No user activity is tracked.
  • No data is sold, shared, or transferred to third parties.

Permissions

Permissions requested by the extension (e.g., access to web pages) are used only to enable core functionality such as highlighting and note-taking, and not for data collection or transmission.

Security

Since all data remains on your device, you maintain full control over its storage and security.

Changes

Any updates to this policy will be reflected with a revised effective date.
