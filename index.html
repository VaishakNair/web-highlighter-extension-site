Highlight or Underline

Choose between classic highlighting or sleek underlining. Pick the style that fits your reading mood.

Order in Chaos

Your highlights automatically follow the page order. If it appears first on the page, it appears first in your list.

Manual Reordering

Move a highlight to the top or group ideas together. Drag and drop to arrange your research exactly how you want.

Layout Preservation

Lists, tables, or complex paragraphs—we keep the original layout so your notes are always easy to read.

Private AI Assistant

Instantly generate unlimited TL;DR summaries or bullet points for any page you read. Since the AI works 100% on your computer, your research stays entirely private and never leaves your device.

Notes & Tags

Add personal notes or use tags to group related ideas. Your digital notebook that lives in the browser.

Dashboard Side Panel

Search, edit, or summarize without leaving the page. A clean dashboard for all your saved snippets.

Full Data Control

Your research belongs to you. Backup or restore data to your device anytime. No servers, no tracking.
